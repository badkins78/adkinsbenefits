What is Mortgage Protection Insurance?

Mortgage protection insurance is a type of insurance policy that is designed to help you pay your mortgage if you become unable to work due to illness, injury, or disability. The policy will typically pay out a monthly benefit for a set period of time (often up to 2 years) to help cover your mortgage payments until you are able to return to work. Some mortgage protection policies may also pay off your entire mortgage balance in the event of your death.

Mortgage protection insurance is different from mortgage insurance, which is a type of insurance that protects the lender in the event that you default on your mortgage payments. Mortgage protection insurance is designed to protect you as the borrower and help ensure that you can continue to make your mortgage payments even if you experience an unexpected loss of income.
