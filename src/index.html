Did You Know

Medicare Supplements Are Standardized

Since 1992, the U.S. Government has mandated that all Medicare Supplement be standardized. That means it does not matter which company you choose for your plan. The coverage is guaranteed to be exactly the same. Once you are on the phone with one of our specialists, you can rest assured you aren't paying too much for your coverage.

And the best part is, in addition to saving money every month on your premium, you do not have to change doctors, or wonder if you are in network. Medicare supplements are nationwide and work exactly the same, no matter what. Let's make sure you aren't paying too much.
